    - Our platform provides two types of vouchers: **Pay-Per-View vouchers** (applicable for a single film) and **Subscription vouchers** (applicable for monthly, quarterly, or yearly subscriptions).

    - |
     **Pay-Per-View Voucher:**

     *Reminder: Pay-Per-View Voucher applicable for a single film only.*
     1. First, log in to the CathayPlay website.
     2. Click on the "**Play Now**" button of a content to purchase it.
     3. Type the "**Voucher Code**" on the right-handed side on the pop-out window.
     4. Click on "**Apply**".
     5. Enjoy the single film after the redemption is successful!

     

     **Subscription Voucher (For Non-Registered User):**

     *Reminder: Subscription Voucher applicable for monthly, quarterly, or yearly subscriptions based on the conditions provided.*
     1. First, log in to the CathayPlay website and click on the "**Register**".
     2. Select your "**Subscription Plan**"
     3. Click on "**Voucher**".
     4. Type the "**Voucher Code**".
     5. Click on "**Apply**".
     6. Enjoy subscription plan after the redemption is successful!

     **Please note:** Make sure to select the corresponding subscription plan before entering the voucher code. For example, if the voucher code is for the *Yearly Subscription Plan*, you must select the *Yearly Subscription Plan* first — otherwise, the code will appear as invalid.



     **Subscription Voucher (For Registered User):**

     *Reminder: Subscription Voucher applicable for monthly, quarterly, or yearly subscriptions based on the conditions provided.*
     1. First, log in to the CathayPlay website.
     2. Click on the "**Play Now**" button of a content to purchase it.
     3. Type the "**Voucher Code**" on the right-handed side on the pop-out window.
     4. Click on "**Apply**".
     5. Enjoy subscription plan after the redemption is successful!
